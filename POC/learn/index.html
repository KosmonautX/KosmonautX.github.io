Pirates of the Collaborean

COLLABORATIVE EXPRESSIVE EXPLORATIVE

Channel the primal drives of your team into a more constructive enterprise

PHASES

5mins

PRE-GAME


Distribute tokens and markers

Fill in agenda cards in line with agendas of the discussion as needed.

Pick an agenda card to start with, then place it on the top corner of the board.

Copy the agenda onto the centre of the board

MOTIVATIONS

Set Overarching Scaffold
Previsualisation of Endgoal

5mins
per agenda

BRAINSTORM

- Plan your points in the Individual Brainstorming Area however you like!

MOTIVATIONS

Socratic Outer Circle
Contemplation
Self reflexive questioning

20mins
per agenda

PLAYSPACE


Start the discussion!

As a group member brings up a new point, they have to place a token on one of the dots in the play zone, while penning it down beside the aforementioned token and drawing a link to the preceding idea

When a group member responds to a point, they have to stack their token on top of the previous one

When a new point is made, a new stack can be started

Irrelevant responses will have their corresponding token fed to the Sea-Monster - an action that can be conducted by any member of the group

After discussion within a stack is concluded, noteworthy insights can be transferred onto the face of the agenda card

MOTIVATIONS

Socratic Inner Circle
Operant Conditioning
Sensemaking
Kinesthetic Learning

10mins
per agenda

POST-ROUND


Once agenda is completed, write the conclusion of the discussion on the back of the agenda card

The card is then placed onto the bottom corner of the board, with the conclusion (bounty) facing up

Rinse and repeat above steps for as many agendas as required

The group can then bring back the agenda cards with consolidated thoughts for each agenda item to conclude play

MOTIVATIONS

Consensus Building: Reflection & Consolidation
